Kwara : Police Arrests Suspected Kidnappers, Ritualists and Fraudsters.

The Kwara State Command of the Nigeria Police has arrested twenty three (23) suspected criminals for various crimes bordering on kidnapping, ritual killing, armed robbery , internet fraud among other criminal acts.

The State Commissioner of Police, Paul Odama further noted that those involved in the kidnap of one Magistrate Jumoke Bamigboye, wife of a former military administrator of Osun and Bauchi States, Theophilus Bamigboye early last year were among the twenty three arrested armed gang.

The command said the suspects were arrested at Eiyenkorin and Bannin forests within the state in conjunction with local vigilante groups following intelligence reports.

Other suspects were arrested for cultism, ritual killings and internet fraud related crimes.

Items recovered from the suspects include , three knives, two cutlasses, charms, one axe, one AK-47 riffle, exotic i-phones and four laptops.

The suspects according to the Commissioner of Police would be charged to court after the conclusion of ongoing investigations .
