YPP and ADP conduct consensus primaries

A private sector business man Dr Nkemdi Okpara has emerged as the Young Progressives Party (YPP) governorship candidate after their primary election held in Owerri the imo State capital.

Announcing the result, the national organising secretary of the party, Vidiyeno Bamaiyi applauded delegates for returning Dr. Okpara unopposed with 145 votes out of 168 delegates and urged them to ensure his success at the election.

Also In a keenly contested primary election held in Owerri, Prof James Okoroma emerged as the Governorship candidate of the African Democratic Congress (ADC).

The Chairman of the Electoral Panel from Abuja, Barr. Emeka Benjamin, announced the election result, declaring Prof Okoroma as the winner with a total of 994 votes to defeat another eminent contestant, Prof. Okey Okoro, who garnered only 715 votes.

The candidates expressed confidence that their party would occupy the state Government House after the governorship election.
