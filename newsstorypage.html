LAGOS: BUILDING COLLAPSE UPDATE

Journalists were on Thursday prevented from gaining access to the site of the banana island 7-storey building collapse by estate security guards stationed at the entrance leading to site of incident. Emergency responders who rushed to the scene shortly after the incident occurred wednesday on their updates said they have since recovered seven persons. The Southwest Coordinator, national Emergency Management Agency, NEMA, Ibrahim Farinloye, while speaking on the latest collapse in the state, noted that no life was lost , as seven persons were rescued alive, and are presently receiving treatments
