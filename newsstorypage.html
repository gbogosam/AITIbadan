Abeokuta: 2023 Rainy season

Ogun state Government has urged residents to regularly listen to weather advisories issued by the state government to avoid disasters during the 2023 rainy season.

Commissioner for environment, Olademeji Oresanya in an exclusive interview told AIT that the state ministry of environment will be giving out information on how to get the best out of this year's rainy season and avert possible hazards that usually come with the season.

The Ogun state commissioner for environment, Olademeji Oresanya said the recent rainfall that flooded parts of sagamu which led to the death of a teenager was due to human error. Oresanya explained that to prevent recurrence , the state government will constantly engage the media to educate the residents and farmers on the 2023 rainy season with a view to advising residents to avoid indiscriminate dumping of wastes across the state.

The commissioner also advised residents to ensure clean air by not burning tires and wastes indiscriminately.

He advised farmers to harvest before October/November to avoid post farming season products been swept away by flood.
