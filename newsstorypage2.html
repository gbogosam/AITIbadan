INEC on Erred staff imprisonment

The Independent National electoral commission is working through the judiciary towards commencing the prosecution and possible imprisonment of its staff found culpable in election infractions before, during and after the 2023 general elections immediately after the conclusion of the supplementary elections.

The Head of INEC KEBBI election monitoring team and the national commissioner of information and voters education, Festus Okoye told (AIT) in Birnin KEBBI that it won't be Bussiness as usual to entertain anymore electoral malpractices as the commission is now compiling names of erred staff for the judicial process to tackle electoral offenses in Nigeria.

At the Post 2023 elections, The Chairman of the Independent National Electoral Commission, Mahmood Yakubu in a meeting with the Resident Electoral commissioned in Abuja had admitted that there are hitches in the conducted polls.
