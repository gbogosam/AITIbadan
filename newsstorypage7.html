KOGI GUBER ASPIRANTS ON WIKE AND DINO

A Kogi State Governorship Aspirant under the platform of the Peoples Democratic Party Bolufemi Rotimi, has backed Rivers State Governor, Nyesome Wike over his comment that former Senator representing Kogi West, Dino Melaye is not fit for governance.

Bolufemi who apologized to the River State Governor over the name calling of Melaye, said any attempt by the party circumvent the process will lead to a total defeat of the party in the state

Bolufemi predicted what he called the final death of PDP if it fails to conduct acceptable primaries.
