INEC ON EBONYI SUPPLEMENTARY ELECTIONS

The Independent National Electoral Commission, INEC in Ebonyi State, Southeast Nigeria says it is set for Saturdayâ€™s supplementary election in some areas of the State, where the February 25 and March 18 exercises were declared inconclusive.

The elections are expected to hold in 74 polling units cutting across seven Local Government Areas, with makeup polls holding for Ezza North/Ishielu Federal Constituency as well as five outstanding State constituency seats.

The State INEC HOD, Voters Education and Publicity, Christian Elekwa, told Journalists in Abakaliki on Friday that the Commission is set for the elections.

Meanwhile, the Ebonyi State Commissioner of Police, CP Faleye Olaleye, has ordered the restriction of all vehicular movements on the roads from 12 midnight on Friday to 6pm on Saturday, across all the affected seven Local Government Areas of the State.

In a statement by the Command's spokesperson, SP. Onome Onovwakpoyeya, the CP is assuring residents of the state of effective collaboration between the police and other sister security agencies to ensure a safe and secure space for citizens of affected areas to freely exercise their franchise.
